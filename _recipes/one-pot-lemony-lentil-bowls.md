difficulty: easy
ingredients:
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil
  - 1 small yellow onion, finely diced
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  - 4 cups vegetable broth
  - 1 tablespoon preserved lemon paste (or finely chopped preserved lemon)
  - 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  - 5 ounces baby spinach
  - 1/2 cup plain yogurt or coconut yogurt, for serving
  - Fresh herbs and lemon wedges, for garnish
steps:
  - Warm the olive oil in a medium pot over medium heat and sauté the onion until translucent, about 4 minutes.
  - Stir in the garlic, lentils, preserved lemon paste, and turmeric; toast for 1 minute until fragrant.
  - Pour in the broth, bring to a gentle boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook uncovered for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  - Fold in the spinach until wilted. Taste and season with salt, adding water if you prefer a soupier bowl.
  - Serve in bowls with yogurt, herbs, and lemon wedges for squeezing.
