ingredients:
  - 4 store-bought naan or flatbreads
  - 1/2 cup jarred pesto
  - 1 cup shredded rotisserie chicken
  - 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  - 1/2 cup halved cherry tomatoes
  - 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  - Crushed red pepper flakes and fresh basil, optional
steps:
  - Heat the broiler to high and line a sheet pan with foil.
  - Spread pesto over each flatbread, then scatter chicken, mozzarella, tomatoes, and red onion evenly.
  - Broil for 5 to 6 minutes, rotating the pan once, until the cheese bubbles and edges toast.
  - Finish with basil and a pinch of red pepper flakes if you like heat.
  - Slice into wedges and serve immediately.
