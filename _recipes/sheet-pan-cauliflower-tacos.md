ingredients:
  - 1 small head cauliflower, chopped into bite-size florets
  - 1 red bell pepper, sliced into strips
  - 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  - 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  - 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  - 8 small tortillas, warmed
  - 1/2 cup prepared lime crema or sour cream mixed with lime zest
  - Toppings: shredded cabbage, pickled onions, fresh cilantro
steps:
  - Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a sheet pan with parchment for quick cleanup.
  - Toss the cauliflower, pepper strips, and black beans with olive oil, cumin, paprika, and salt directly on the pan.
  - Roast for 15 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the cauliflower edges char and the beans crisp slightly.
  - Warm the tortillas while the tray finishes and set out bowls for toppings.
  - Spoon the roasted vegetables onto tortillas, drizzle with lime crema, and finish with cabbage, pickled onions, and cilantro.
notes: |
  Swap in chickpeas or frozen corn if that's what you have—Carl automatically adjusts the roasting time based on moisture.
